# A.13 — Emotional Awareness (Biological Signals Matter for System Design)

## The Principle

Human emotional states are measurable biological phenomena,
not abstract qualities. Systems designed for sustained
human interaction must account for these biological states
because they affect how the human interacts with the system.

| Biological State | Measurable Signal | System Response |
|-----------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Stress | Cortisol elevation, shortened inputs, erratic pacing | Session protocol check-in, potential stasis |
